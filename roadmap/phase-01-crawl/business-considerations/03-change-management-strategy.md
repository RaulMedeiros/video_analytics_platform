# Phase 1 Change Management Strategy
## Technology Adoption Framework - CRAWL Phase

---

## 🎯 Change Management Overview

Phase 1 introduces **significant technological change** that requires careful management of organizational adoption. The strategy focuses on **minimizing disruption** while **maximizing technology adoption** through structured change management and comprehensive stakeholder engagement.

### **Change Management Objectives**
- **Smooth Technology Transition**: Seamless adoption of video analytics platform
- **User Acceptance**: High user adoption and satisfaction rates
- **Capability Building**: Development of required technical and operational skills
- **Cultural Alignment**: Integration with existing organizational culture
- **Success Measurement**: Clear metrics for change management effectiveness
