# Phase 3 Global Talent Excellence
## Worldwide Technology Leadership - RUN Phase

---

## 🎯 Global Talent Strategy Overview

Phase 3 establishes a **world-class global talent organization** that attracts, develops, and retains the world's top technology talent. The strategy focuses on **talent excellence**, **global leadership development**, **innovation culture**, and **sustainable competitive advantage** through human capital excellence.

### **Global Talent Objectives**
- **Talent Excellence**: World's top technology talent acquisition and retention
- **Global Leadership**: Comprehensive global leadership development program
- **Innovation Culture**: Culture of innovation, excellence, and continuous learning
- **Competitive Advantage**: Sustained competitive advantage through talent superiority
- **Organizational Excellence**: High-performance global organization capabilities
